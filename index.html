Yoshi! Hoo!

Yoshi is an innocent, dinosaur-like creature from Dinosaur Land who was trapped in an egg by Bowser's forces but was rescued by Mario and Luigi during their vacation to Dinosaur Land to save Princess Peach. Upon hatching, Yoshi informs the brothers of Bowser's actions and the plight of his friends, who were also captured and sealed in eggs by the Koopa tribe. Together, the trio embarks on a quest to rescue Yoshi's captured friends and the Princess, with Yoshi serving as Mario's mount and ally
